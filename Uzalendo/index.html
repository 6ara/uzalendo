Our mission is to promote transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership in all aspects of governance in Kenya. At our core, we believe that every citizen has the right to access information, participate in decision-making processes, and hold leaders accountable. We work towards achieving this by providing education, research, and advocacy to empower citizens to demand their rights.

OUR METHODOLOGY

We use a multi-faceted strategy including research,advocacy, and pro bono legal aid to achieve our mission. Our pro bono lawyers provide aid to citizens who face legal barriers in holding leaders accountable. This helps to ensure equal access to justice and level the playing field. Our advocacy for Good Governance and Constitutionalism in Kenya includes protecting citizens' rights.
